Review incoming client intake form and route the lead:

1. **Classify Service Tier** — Map to Concept / Builder / Concierge / White Glove based on scope, budget, and timeline signals
2. **Score Urgency** — Flag time-sensitive projects (permit deadlines, seasonal planting windows, construction dependencies)
3. **Validate Completeness** — Check intake form for missing fields; request follow-up from client-comms if needed
4. **Assign PM** — Assign project-manager and hand off intake data for PRD creation

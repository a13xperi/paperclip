Generate plant palette for a specific project's conditions:

1. Pull project site conditions — climate zone, sun exposure, soil type, water restrictions
2. Query archive for plants used in similar conditions across past projects
3. Filter by client style preferences and budget constraints
4. Assemble palette with species, spacing, water needs, and seasonal interest notes
5. Deliver palette to project record for design team review

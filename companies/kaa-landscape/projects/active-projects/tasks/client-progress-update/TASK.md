Prepare and send weekly client-facing progress updates:

1. **Pull Project Status** — Get current milestone status and recent activity from project-manager's weekly review
2. **Draft Update** — Summarize completed work, in-progress items, and upcoming milestones in client-friendly language
3. **Flag Decisions Needed** — Highlight any pending client decisions or approvals blocking progress
4. **Attach Visuals** — Include relevant design renders, site photos, or progress shots where available
5. **Send & Log** — Deliver update to client and log to project record

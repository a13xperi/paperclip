Review deliverables before they advance to the next milestone:

1. **Zoning Compliance** — Verify setbacks, lot coverage, height limits, and use restrictions
2. **ADA Review** — Check accessible path of travel, ramp grades, surface materials
3. **MWELO Check** — Validate water budget, hydrozoning, and irrigation efficiency against California MWELO requirements
4. **Fire Safety** — Review defensible space, plant spacing, and material choices in fire-prone zones
5. **PRD Conformance** — Confirm deliverable matches the approved design brief, scope, and budget
6. **Issue Verdict** — Pass / Conditional (with required fixes) / Fail (with findings and remediation steps)

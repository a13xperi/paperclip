KAA Landscape is a San Francisco Bay Area landscape architecture firm with a 10,000+ project archive spanning residential and commercial design. The agent company automates the full project lifecycle from client intake through plan delivery.

KAA operates as a hub-and-spoke organization. The CEO triages incoming work across four service tiers and delegates to specialists. The Project Manager owns milestones and PRD hygiene. The Design Archivist searches the archive for plant palettes, precedent imagery, and material specifications. The QA Reviewer gates all deliverables for building code, ADA, and MWELO compliance. Client Comms drafts all client-facing communications with human review before sending.

## Service Tiers

| Tier | Price | Scope |
|------|-------|-------|
| Concept | $299 | Initial design concept and plant palette |
| Builder | Custom | Full plans for contractor handoff |
| Concierge | Custom | Design + project management through installation |
| White Glove | Custom | End-to-end design, installation oversight, and maintenance planning |

## Workflow

1. The **CEO** triages intake signals and assigns to the appropriate service tier
2. The **Project Manager** creates the PRD, sets milestones, and coordinates the team
3. The **Design Archivist** researches precedents, plant palettes, and material options from the archive
4. The **QA Reviewer** validates plans against building codes, ADA, MWELO, and budget
5. **Client Comms** drafts all client-facing updates, proposals, and milestone notifications
